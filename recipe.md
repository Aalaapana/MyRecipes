
STEP 1 
Add crushed garlic cloves into the salted butter and mix.

STEP 2
Take a bread silce and apply the garlic butter on it.

STEP 3
Then add cheese (add any vegetables like capsicum, corn if needed )and close it with an another slice of bread

STEP 4
heat up the pan and rost the bread until it is golden brown on both sides.

STEP 5
add additional oregano or chill flakes if needed on the garlic bread

SERVE HOT AND ENJOY!!
